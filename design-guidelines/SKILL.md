# Standards Consultants — Design Guidelines

The canonical reference is the **Standards Corporate Profile 2026 (15 Years of Excellence)**. Match its tone, layout, and density before finalizing any presentation, proposal, or formal report.

## Color system

| Role | Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Primary | Standards Navy | `#202040` | 32·32·64 | Wordmark, body type, primary surfaces |
| Accent | Standards Gold | `#D0A030` | 208·160·48 | The "S" square, statistics, accent rules, CTA highlights |
| Secondary | Action Blue | `#1565C0` | 21·101·192 | Illustrations, iconography, secondary callouts |
| Body | Slate | `#5A6173` | 90·97·115 | Body copy on light surfaces |
| Background | Paper | `#F6F7FA` | 246·247·250 | Default ground for content pages |

**Usage ratio (per 100):** Paper 50 · Navy 30 · Gold 10 · Blue 6 · Slate 4.

## Typography

- **Display & UI:** Montserrat (700–800 for headings, 600 for eyebrow/UI labels).
- **Body:** Open Sans (400 default, 600 for lead/emphasis).
- Headings tightly tracked; never more than two weights per heading.
- Body line-height 1.5.
- Eyebrow style: 7–8 px Montserrat 700, letter-spacing .22em, UPPERCASE, in Gold.

## Logo system

- Primary mark: custom Montserrat Black wordmark in Navy, leading "S" framed by a Gold square outline. Symbolizes "set apart from standard."
- Clear space = height of the gold square ("X") on every side.
- Min reproduction: 28 mm in print · 110 px on screen.
- Variants: Primary (white bg) · Reverse (navy bg) · On cream · On gold.
- **Never:** distort, stretch, recolor, add drop-shadow, or re-typeset.

## Presentation / document format playbook

The Corporate Profile is structured as a sequence of page types. Use them in this order for any new presentation, proposal, or formal report.

### Page types

1. **Cover page** — split layout
   - Left half on Paper: massive Gold numerals ("20·26") · concise eyebrow ("15 YEARS OF EXCELLENCE") · Navy h1 ("CORPORATE PROFILE" or document title).
   - Right half: full-bleed photo with Navy duotone overlay.
   - Bottom-left: STANDARDS logo.

2. **Section divider** — full-bleed Navy
   - Centered logo at top.
   - Short Gold rule.
   - Three-word phrase in Gold ("HEAD - HEART - HAND" or "TRUST - CULTURE - OWNERSHIP").
   - Optional attribution below ("- Hady Safa, Chief Executive Officer").

3. **Title-left / list-right content page** — Paper background
   - Gold rule above title (left column).
   - Title in Navy, Montserrat 700, large.
   - Optional subtitle in Navy below.
   - Right column: bulleted or unbulleted list, each item Navy, generous line-height.
   - Header strip: relevant photo with Navy duotone overlay.

4. **Icon grid** — Navy background
   - Title at top in white, Gold rule below.
   - 3×3 grid of items, each: icon tile (alternating white-on-Navy and Gold-on-white) + label in white.
   - Used for service categories, client segments.

5. **Stat-hero** — full-bleed Navy with Gold accent strip on left
   - Lead phrase in white ("DURING THE LAST 14 YEARS, WE ARE PROUD TO HAVE A RATIO OF").
   - Massive Gold percentage ("93%") — Montserrat 800, ~200 px.
   - Sub-label in white ("SATISFACTION RATE OF LEARNING OUTCOME").
   - Bottom: short Gold rule, optional explanatory body.

6. **Logo wall** — Navy background
   - Section title in white, centered.
   - Client logos in white circular frames, evenly spaced.
   - Group by industry vertical with sub-header bands.

7. **Footer / contact page** — full-bleed photo with Navy overlay
   - Centered logo.
   - Address line · phone · email.
   - Sober, no other ornament.

### Format rules

- **All-caps section labels** for chapter titles.
- **Three-word taglines** for chapter dividers (e.g. "Research – Customization – Excellence").
- **One headline + one phrase + one paragraph** per content page; never crowd.
- **Photos always Navy duotone** — never full-color stock.
- **Statistics shown massive** — three to five digits max, Montserrat 800, Gold.
- **No drop-shadows, no gradients, no decorative emoji, no rounded photo corners.**

### Output format mapping

- **Slide deck (PPTX):** match the page types 1:1 — cover, divider, content, icon grid, stat, logo wall, contact.
- **Proposal (PDF/Word):** cover + executive-summary divider + needs analysis (title-left/list-right) + program structure (icon grid) + outcomes (stat-hero) + investment table + terms + contact footer.
- **Internal report (Markdown):** structured headings reflecting the same hierarchy; render to PDF using these colors and fonts when sharing externally.

### Stock-language phrases that work

When you need a brand-aligned phrase mid-document, prefer these patterns over generic copy:

- "Trusted partner since [year]."
- "Tailored to your local culture."
- "Practical content, interactive delivery."
- "Research · Customization · Excellence."
- "Trust · Culture · Ownership."
- "We help individuals become their best version."
- "Decisions make destinies."
